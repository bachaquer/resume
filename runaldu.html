My story

Here is an additional story about my life that could give you a basis for my achievements and experiences

Story of My Life

From early childhood, I was into mathematics and sciences. The years passed, but I wasn't realising how easy and interesting these subjects were to me. I attended some regional mathematics competitions and was awarded by several diplomas, but even then I didn't know how this would help me in life and how people use it in their own life.

It was the moment when I entered Atyrau "Bilim-Innovation" High School when I realised that every subject has its own purpose. Students there were participating in many olympiads and living their life. At that time, I decided to enter the Olympiad team by myself. Unfortunately, at the start, I failed one math and logic examination which would decide which olympiad I would go to and whether I would be accepted. I was partially sad that I wouldn't be able to study in the field of my interest and made myself believe that sciences are not my field Then, I decided to enter English Olympiad Team to develop my English Language at least.

To my own surprise, I liked being a part of such a great community, but I was always asking myself questions like what if I were in the other olympiad team and so on. On the one hand, I was feeling that Computer Science is my field and that I could deal with the difficulties of coding. On the other hand, I was afraid of being rejected again and that I could never catch up with my peers who were in the olympiad for several years. I was also feeling that my years spent in English Olympiad would be a loss if I left the Olympiad Team. While I was deciding, a lot of time passed.

At the end, I came to the conclusion that it is better late than never, better to go for your dreams, never look back, never regret anything from the past and live, being happy. I left the English Olympiad and entered the Informatics Olympiad. At first, it was morally hard for me to study the basics with the students who were about 3 years younger than me. I spent days and nights learning algorithms and structures. As a result, I got the bronze medal in Republican Olympiad that year. I feel really proud not only for achieving that medal but also for leaving my own comfort zone and becoming more active in my life. Then, I started participating in many other activities, trying new things and finding my new hobbies. One of such, to illustrate, is my hobby of creating web designs.
